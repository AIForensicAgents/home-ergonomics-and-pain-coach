📚 Quick Reference

💻 Desk Setup Essentials

  • • Screen at eye level, arm's length away
  • • Elbows at 90° angle when typing
  • • Feet flat on floor, knees at 90°
  • • Monitor 20-26 inches from eyes
  • • Use a separate keyboard when using laptop

👶 Baby Carrier Adjustments

  • • Waist belt should sit on hip bones, not waist
  • • Baby's head should be kiss-height
  • • Shoulder straps snug but not digging in
  • • Switch sides frequently when hip-carrying
  • • Tighten back panel to keep baby close

🛏️ Crib Height Guide

  • • Mattress at your hip height for newborns
  • • Lower when baby can sit (about 6mo)
  • • Lowest setting when baby can stand
  • • Bend at knees, not waist, when reaching in
  • • Drop the side rail before lifting baby out

🏋️ Safe Lifting Technique

  • • Get close to your child before lifting
  • • Bend at knees and hips, keep back straight
  • • Engage core before lifting
  • • Hold child close to your body
  • • Avoid twisting — pivot with feet instead
